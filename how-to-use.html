📘 Complete Guide: How to Use the LLTS App

👤 Step 1: Register Your Account

LLTS lets you register as an Owner (who posts tenders) or a Worker (who applies for work). Use your phone number to create an account, and add basic details like your name, location, and skills (for workers).

  • ✅ Same number can be used to create both roles.
  • 📌 Owner Example: Plumber needing electric rewiring.
  • 📌 Worker Example: Electrician ready to take small jobs nearby.

🔐 Step 2: Login and Access Your Dashboard

Log in using your role-specific credentials. Each role has a dedicated dashboard:

  • Owner Dashboard: Post tenders, view applicants, accept/reject applications, and manage tokens.
  • Worker Dashboard: Explore tenders, apply, view job status, and manage your wallet.

📋 Step 3: Post or Apply to a Tender

Everything in LLTS revolves around the Tender and Auction system. Here's how it works:

For Owners

  • 📝 Fill in job title, description, expected deadline, and budget.
  • 📍 Include your location and category (e.g., plumbing, electrician, student work).
  • 🧾 Tender is shown to workers of that category.

For Workers

  • 🔍 Browse tenders that match your category and location.
  • 📨 Click “Apply” to submit your interest with a custom message.

🤝 Step 4: Accepting Applicants & Collaboration

Owners can review incoming applications, view worker profiles, and accept the most suitable person.

  • ✅ Once accepted, the worker is notified instantly.
  • 📱 A WhatsApp or in-app chat can be initiated to coordinate work.
  • 🔒 The application is locked after acceptance to prevent spamming.

🎟️ Understanding the Token System

The LLTS economy runs on tokens to ensure commitment and reduce spam.

  • 💰 New users get 10 free tokens during signup.
  • 📤 Posting a tender costs 3 tokens.
  • 📥 Applying for a job also costs 3 tokens.
  • 🛒 You can purchase tokens anytime from your dashboard.

Why Tokens? To filter out fake users and ensure that only serious jobs/applications get posted.

⚠️ Platform Ethics & Safety Rules

  • 🚫 No fake tenders or prank applications allowed — permanent ban applies.
  • 📵 Users must be respectful and follow proper language during conversations.
  • 🔍 All jobs are location-aware — posting a tender outside your city may lead to low response.
  • 🧾 Signing the LLTS Promise Document is recommended for bigger jobs to avoid disputes.
