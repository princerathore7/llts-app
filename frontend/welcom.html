What is LLTS?

LLTS (Local Livelihood Tender System) is a platform designed to connect local workers, job seekers, students, and small business owners across India. Whether you're an electrician, plumber, painter, or a student posting an assignment job, LLTS offers a reliable system for tender posting and work completion.

Our mission is to support grassroots employment and make tender-based work accessible and secure. LLTS enables direct connection between owners and workers with no middleman, and fosters a trusted, community-driven work ecosystem.
