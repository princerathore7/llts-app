🚩 Report a Tender

Please make sure your report contains:

  • 🔹 Valid reason (e.g. fake tender, scam, abusive behavior)
  • 🔹 Screenshots of chat (WhatsApp or app)
  • 🔹 Mention of tender title or tender ID
  • 🔹 Owner name or ID if possible

Examples of reportable issues:

  • ❌ Tender created but owner doesn't respond or denies giving work
  • ❌ Owner used offensive language
  • ❌ Promoting illegal/nudity/inappropriate content
  • ❌ Misleading or fake budget promises
Note: Admin will verify every report manually within 48 hours.
